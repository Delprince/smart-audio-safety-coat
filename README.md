# smart-audio-safety-coat.
A digital product concept: a wearable safety coat with an integrated bluetooth audio and hands-free communication for construction workers. project 4A
the smart audio safety coat is a wearable solution made for construction workers who need safety, convinience and hands-free audio during work. this product provide open ear audio directly from a durable safety coat, which will improve productivity and communication on site
construction environment are stressful, loud and communication-intensive. workers often rely on music to stay active/motivated, but earphone/earpod pose safety risk because they break easily. this coat will solve these issues by integrating audio into clothing. no earphones needed.
